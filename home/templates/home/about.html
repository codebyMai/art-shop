About the Artist

Maria Zawilska is a highly skilled oil painter with over 30 years of experience in the world of fine art. Throughout her extensive career, she has developed a unique artistic voice, blending classical techniques with a modern sensibility. Her works often explore themes of human emotion, nature, and the delicate interplay between light and shadow. Maria’s mastery of oil painting allows her to create rich textures and vivid color palettes that bring depth and life to her subjects.

Her commitment to her craft has earned her recognition in both national and international art circles, with her pieces being displayed in galleries and exhibitions across the globe. With a deep understanding of the tradition of oil painting, Maria Zawilska continues to evolve as an artist, producing compelling, thought-provoking works that resonate with audiences worldwide.

Maria Zawilska’s technique as a traditional oil painter is deeply rooted in the classical methods of painting, which she has honed and refined over her 30-year career. Her work focuses primarily on detailed landscapes and still lives, where her expertise in capturing natural light, texture, and intricate detail is evident. For her landscapes, Maria typically begins by observing her surroundings, either painting en plein air or using photographs as references. She meticulously studies the interplay of light, atmosphere, and natural elements, translating them into her compositions with a careful layering process. She applies thin layers of oil paint, gradually building up depth and richness. Using the glazing technique, she creates transparent layers that allow the light to penetrate and reflect through, enhancing the sense of luminosity and realism in her work.

In her still life paintings, Maria is known for her precision and attention to minute details. She arranges everyday objects—often flowers, fruits, or vases—with great care, capturing the subtleties of texture, light, and shadow. She employs fine brushwork to depict delicate surfaces, whether it’s the glossy sheen of a fruit, the soft petals of a flower, or the reflective quality of glass or metal.

Throughout her practice, Maria remains true to the traditional approach of using an underpainting to establish values and tones, then gradually building up color and detail. Her final layers emphasize highlights and small details, lending her paintings a luminous, almost lifelike quality. Her careful blending and attention to texture give her works an immersive, three-dimensional quality that draws viewers into the scene.

Maria Zawilska’s traditional oil painting technique reflects her deep understanding of both the medium and the subjects she paints. Her landscapes and still lifes are not only skillfully rendered but also infused with a sense of timeless beauty and reverence for the natural world.
